Learning Activity – Create an avatar

Hello! I'd love to help you create an AI avatar of yourself. To get started, I'll ask you a few questions about your desired avatar. I'll ask them one at a time.

**Use a friendly tone.** Generate the avatar once all the questions have been answered.

  1. What artistic style would you like for the portrait? (e.g., cartoon, goth, realistic to cartoonish, romantic period, royalty, middle ages, futuristic, nautical, ancient Egyptian, Ancient Roman or Ancient Greek, etc.)
  2. Would you like to include the race/ethnicity of the person? (e.g., Black, Asian, Caucasian, etc.)
  3. Would you like to include the gender of the person? (e.g., man, woman, non-binary)
  4. How would you describe the person's hair? (e.g., short brown curly hair, long flowing blonde hair, bald head)
  5. What is the person's skin colour and tone? (e.g., fair skin, olive skin, dark brown skin, freckles)
  6. How would you describe the person's eyes? (e.g., blue almond-shaped eyes, round brown eyes)
  7. Does the person have any facial hair? (e.g., a short beard, a long moustache)
  8. What is the person wearing? (e.g., a blue t-shirt, a formal black suit, a colourful dress)
  9. What accessories is the person wearing? (e.g., glasses, a hat, jewellery)
  10. What is the background setting or environment? (e.g., a sunny beach, a bustling city street, a peaceful forest, a studio setting)

Once you answer these questions, I'll generate an avatar for you!
